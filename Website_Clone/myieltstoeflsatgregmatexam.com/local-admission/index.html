GETTING LOCAL ADMISSION
We help our students to secure admissions into Private, State and Federal Institutions in Nigeria; but it is important for you to know that our strength is in Private Institutions where admission processing is less tedious and transparent. We process admissions ranging from Secondary to Tertiary level of studies.

SECONDARY LEVEL
Students will have to pass through the following steps in order to secure admission into a secondary school:
1. Complete a registration form
2. Take a pre – admission entrance test
3. Oral interview
4. Counseling/advise
5. Deployment into a suitable school
We advise you professionally on best options in terms of school to choose and courses to take so as to excel in the long run; although parents and guidance have the final say.

 

TERTIARY LEVEL
Students seeking admission into any tertiary institution in Nigeria must have written and passed either WAEC or NECO or both in addition to having a good score in the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME). This is why we take our students through a complete process of Counseling, Exam Registration, Exam Coaching, Exam Attendance and finally getting the Admission. Note that it’s a holistic process. Therefore to secure local admission through Unique Edu Consult, students must undergo the following compulsory exercises:
1. Complete a registration form
2. Take a pre – admission entrance test
3. Oral interview
4. Counseling/advise
5. Register and coach the student for exams such as WAEC, NECO and JAMB (if not already done)
6. Register and coach the student for Post – UTME exams – Change of course/institution (if necessary)
7. Final Admission Letter
The entire process can be solved mathematically if 100% left in our hands. We cannot secure admission for a student with poor result, which is why we advise parents to leave the entire process to us for easy accountability. To secure local admission through Unique Edu Consult, students can either fill up an online Student Enquiry Form here or visit our office for direct consultation.
